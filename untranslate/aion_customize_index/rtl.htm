AionianBible.org RTL and Hindic Language Proofer Reader

Right-to-left and Hindic languages require additional proof reading in the Aionian Bible project. The reason for this is that the Speedata PDF authoring software could not handle the glyph placement for these languages until recently. The Harfbuzz module was added to manage right-to-left and Hindic language glyph placement. However, since this feature is in Beta development additional proof reading is needed until success is fully confirmed. Please contact Jeff at Nainoia Inc with comments and questions.
